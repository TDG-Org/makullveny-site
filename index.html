A calm study room for your computer

Makullveny helps students write, organize, and study without losing their peace.

Makullveny is a local-first desktop study assistant in active development. It starts with a cozy notes workspace built around books, pages, drawing, ambience, and a lofi radio, then grows into focused study tools that reduce busywork.

What it is

A student productivity app with a beautiful notes desk at the center.

Library Desk

Create books, bookshelves, and pages for classes, study journals, and projects. Notes can include typed text, drawings, imported images, and page customization.

Calm Study Atmosphere

Ambient sounds, a lofi radio, warm themes, and a cozy cabin workspace help make long study sessions feel less harsh and more intentional.

Study Tools

Makullveny is being prepared for helpful modules like flashcards, class setup helpers, quick references, and student-friendly workflows.

Why it exists

Less context switching. More useful study time.

Students often bounce between notes, PDFs, calculators, converters, music, class pages, and search tabs. Makullveny is being built to bring more of that study flow into one calm place.

The goal is not to replace every school tool. The goal is to make the student feel more organized, less scattered, and better supported while working.

Privacy

Local-first by default.

Notes stay local. Books, pages, drawings, and study content are stored on the user's computer unless the user chooses to export or move data.

Payments are external. If support payments are enabled, Stripe handles the checkout page. Makullveny does not collect or store card numbers.

Accounts are future optional work. The app is being designed so core study features can remain useful without forcing cloud sign-in.

Support

Support Makullveny development.

Support payments help fund development time, design polish, testing, audio/theme improvements, and future student tools. Support is optional and is not required to use the core local app.
